HOW IT ALL STARTED

Having bored her friends for years about the expensive prices of amiibo, accessories and figures in London, our founder Isabella finally decided to do something about it. With plans to start a small store which would initially offer figures and accessories from the community at a good price, she took the idea to crowdfunding platform Kickstarter & 256 backers made that dream come true. Each of their names are still displayed proudly on the back window of that tiny shop, which the backers affectionately christened ‘Manzil Saghir’.

The first figure was brewed in Shoreditch on the 3rd November 2014. Many years later and thanks to the support of our many wonderful customers, friends, partners and suppliers, Supreme Small Box has grown to a company that has a wonderful community, as well as a large chain of stores that offer the best service at the best price.
